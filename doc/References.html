Introduction

Addressing support questions on dar-support mailing list, I discovered unexected use case of dar/libdar, both impressive and amazing. I thus asked there, who wanted to present their particular use cases to be used as reference for dar/libdar and then selected the few that really worth mentionning!

As this mailing-list is far from being read by all dar/libdar users, you may well have missed my request there. But if you have an original usage of dar/libdar, in term of backup size, media, environment, purpose, tools... and would like to be added to this list, you are welcome! Just let me know! [1]

This is thus just the beginning of this list...!

Denis Corbin
